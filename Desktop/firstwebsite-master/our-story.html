Our Farm

Westfalia Farms is a 90-acres farm on rolling green hills. Farmers by heart and training, we try an organic and fresh approach to farming, our animals and land. We raise and breed organic grass-fed Belted Galloway cattle, organic free-range chickens, Tennessee walking horses, produce organic hay and manage USDA conservation land with abundant wildlife including a growing quail population.

The farm is operated by the Knoeppel Family, originally from Westfalia in Germany. The pretty rolling green hills, ponds and lakes of beautiful Paris, TN reminded us of our hills back home in “Westfalen” - that is where the name of the farm came from.
