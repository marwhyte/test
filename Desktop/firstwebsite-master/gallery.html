BELTED GALLOWAY

Our farm is home to Belted Galloway, an ancient breed of Galloway cattle. Belted Galloway are slow-growing and robust and do particularly well when pasture-raised and grass-fed. They produce high quality beef, lean and with excellent fat and nutrient composition. They look good and are quite docile and easy to handle! Discover more.

FINE SELECTION

We are producing pasture-raised, grass-fed, grass-finished steers for direct “farm to table” sales. If you are interested, contact us. We are currently expanding our herd of registered purebred Belted Galloway cattle.
